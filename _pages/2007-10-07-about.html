It is always a great relief to find an article on a web by someone who has already been there and made it work. In these very considerations flash tekkie is introducing various Flash development issues and, first and foremost, fixes and workarounds.

Alongside the Flash development articles there is a whole spectrum of topics from various fields of IT and, why not, computer arts for instance.
