On Friday Telia announced iPhone3G prices for Sweden. It will be available in all 84 Swedish Telia stores with 3 subscription options, iMini, iMidi and iMaxi, offering the cheapest iPhone ever for 1 SEK (0.11€)!

Telia's EDGE-network covers approximately 90% of Sweden while the HSPA network is available for almost 6 million inhabitants in Sweden. Telia's customers also have access to over 1 800 WLAN-hotspots in the country.

In addition, as there's still a lot of uncertainty about the launch of the new iPhone in Baltics (except that it's coming this summer), TeliaSonera's Estonian branch EMT has started with pre-registrations that do not obligate you to purchase but keeps you on the frontline of freshest news about the arrival of iPhone, sales terms etc.
