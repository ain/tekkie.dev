Vodafone partner in Estonia, Elisa, claims its mobile internet usage has grown approximately 11 times in the first half of 2007.

99% of Estonia's population lives in the coverage area of Elisa. 65% of the territory has EDGE connectivity with mobile connection speeds up to 240 Kbps and approximately 30% of population lives in the 3.5G (HSDPA) coverage area enabling speeds up to 3.6 Mbps.

The growth of mobile internet usage is considered to be based on the expansion to remote areas, EDGE availability and reasonable pricing. M-Internet package with unlimited traffic is currently priced at around 15 EUR monthly. Day-ticket for M-Internet costs around 1 EUR and is valid for 12h.
