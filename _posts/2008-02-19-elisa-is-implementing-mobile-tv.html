2 months after the launch of the first commercial UMTS900 network in November 2007, Elisa launched UMTS900 network in Estonia and is now aiming to implement Mobile TV on top of it.

Mobile TV platform will be delivered by Rubberduck Media Lab, the leading technology expert in mobile broadcasting of Scandinavia. Based in Oslo, Norway, Rubberduck co-operates with Telenor, TeliaSonera, Hi3G Sweden & Denmark, BBC, MTV and Fox so the platform will bring a lot of domestic and global content.
